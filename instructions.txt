Welcome user, these are the insructions 
regarding adding a new contact to the 
catalog.

1.Name and Number is a mandatory field
2.If you choose not to fill any of the 
  optional fields, then leave it blank
3.In the category section,you can write 
  Family,Friends,Work,Others.If 
  unfilled the section is stored as 
  Others
4.For DOB the format is dd-mm-yyyy,
  Use '-' and not '/', else it shows
  invalid
5.Number section should only contain 
  numbers else it shows invalid

Feel free to add as many contacts as 
you want
